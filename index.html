Chickens are amazing!

Chickens are often just seen as food but are actually very intelligent. They are able to recognize up to 100 faces, animal or human alike!

The term "pecking order" comes from the heirarchy chickens utilize for social organization. They literally peck one another in a show of strength. The stronger, more dominant chickens will ascend to the top of the pecking order whereas the weaker will be at the bottom and often will run away.
